Hey there, and thank you for checking out my artwork :3 I enjoy putting my all in the art that I create.

Brianna Laurice Gregory is from Camden, NJ. She is a woman with strong ambitions and a child's sense of imagination. She's worked on music videos as a rotoscoped animator and is currently a children book lllustrator. Currently, Brianna is an Honor Student at Morgan State University and will be receiving her Bachelor's degree in Fine Arts in May of 2021. Her all time dream is to work at Cartoon Network, Nickelodeon, and Disney Animation Studios as a storyboard artist/character designer.  For art inquires, Brianna can be contacted at artcentricbri@gmail.com or via Instagram @artcentricbri.
